Waste less, store safe, save lives

What we do

Our mission is to not allow any medicine going to third world and developing countries to be wasted. We will build centers in counties of need to store the medicine. We will also have custom vans to deliver the medicine to places in need.

We are going to build our center in the capital city Gitega of the country Burundi. Burundi is a country needing medical help, because it is a developing country at the moment and there are less then 0.5 doctors for 10k inhabitants

We have chosen exactly this place, because it is in the middle of Africa and most of the African countries are needing medical help. We are going to export the medicine from Burundi, so it is not that far to other countries needing help.
